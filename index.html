Jam with Jamendo

Welcome to the webpage of the prototype Jam with Jamendo, a web-based proof-of-concept prototype that promotes the practice of known chords with suitable songs from the Jamendo online database.

See below two video demos related to two conference publications and a live demo.

JwJ demo Audio Mostly 2018

This demo showcases the version of the web app Jam with Jamendo that we used in the paper:
Anna Xambó, Johan Pauwels, Gerard Roma, Mathieu Barthet, and György Fazekas. 2018. "Jam with Jamendo: Querying a Large Music Collection by Chords from a Learner’s Perspective". In Audio Mostly 2018: Sound in Immersion and Emotion (AM’18), September 12–14, 2018, Wrexham, United Kingdom. ACM, New York, NY, USA, 7 pages.
